Day 1: Classroom English

Student worksheet, phrase cards, listening check, and teacher notes.

Phrase cardCan you repeat that, please?

Use when you did not hear.

Phrase cardI don’t understand.

Use when the meaning is unclear.

Phrase cardCan you help me?

Use when you need support.

Phrase cardI’m ready.

Use when you can begin.

Phrase cardI have a question.

Use before asking.

Phrase cardPlease.

Use to be polite.
